Explore Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan's capital, is a vibrant and fascinating city that offers a unique blend of traditional and modern culture, rich history, and cutting-edge technology. As a tourist destination, Tokyo has something for everyone.

Top Places to Visit in Tokyo

Shibuya Crossing

One of the busiest intersections in the world, known for its incredible energy.

Senso-ji Temple

Tokyo's oldest temple, a stunning example of traditional Japanese architecture.

Travel Tips for Tokyo

Food

Don't miss sushi, ramen, and street food at Tsukiji Fish Market.

Best Season to Visit

Spring (for cherry blossoms) and Fall are beautiful and pleasant.

Visitor Experiences

"Tokyo is an exciting mix of tradition and modernity; the temples and neon lights are a surreal combination." - Visitor
